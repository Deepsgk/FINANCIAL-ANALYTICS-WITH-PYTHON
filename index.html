The Perfect

The zephyr tempt to bribe the winged,

ecstasy finds its destination Far away in the empyrean.

A wish remains unanswered

to accept the invitation of the cajoling waves,

to its empire ruled by the finned where the sun denies to set. Even to envy is the firefly who plays hide and seek with the dark.

these fluvial desires could never meet the sea.

to ostracise the rest and demarcate the ‘Perfect’ Out ;

bestowed the rights.

For, Every creation of mine is a masterpiece says ‘The Almighty’.

The nearest, who embraces whatever I gave and utilises it the best.

one who enfolds the mystic spell of love and uplifts rest to explore contentment,

Is on which I embark my autograph.

Otiose deeds and words of sympathy take them away from me.

For, never shower sympathy, if cannot rhyme deeds with words.

The endearment becomes the greatest Enormity.

Not to leave the ‘Perfect’ undefined

‘I’the supreme establish

He who lives beyond demarcation and Respect my every creation As Divine gift is

‘The Perfect’.
